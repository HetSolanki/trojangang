What is Astrology

Astrology is an ancient practice that explores the connection between celestial movements and human lives. Rooted in the belief that the positions of stars and planets influence personality traits, emotions, and life events, astrology provides insights into self-discovery and guidance. It is often associated with zodiac signs, birth charts, and horoscopes, offering a unique perspective on personal growth, relationships, and decision-making. While some view it as a science of cosmic alignment, others see it as a tool for reflection and spiritual connection. Regardless of belief, astrology continues to captivate and inspire people worldwide.
